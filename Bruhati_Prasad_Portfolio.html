About me

A PM who actually reads the docs

Hey! I'm Bruhati — a product manager based in Bengaluru who specialises in healthcare technology. I've spent the last 3 years at Circle Health building things that matter: the Circle Health consumer app for consultations and lab bookings, dashboards that help care teams spot at-risk patients faster, an in-house WhatsApp communication platform, and EHR systems that clinicians actually want to use.

I've also delivered for international clients — including an AI-powered medical data extractor built for a healthcare client in the Philippines. My work spans consumer products, internal tools, and US-market care management programs.

My superpower is sitting at the intersection of clinical complexity and clean product thinking. I speak fluent HIPAA, can write acceptance criteria in my sleep, and genuinely enjoy a well-run sprint retrospective. I'm currently pursuing my MBA at Manipal University Jaipur while working full-time — because apparently I love a challenge.
